    How to Play

    Goal Fill all 10 slots on your board before your opponent.
    Setup Each player gets 10 face-down cards in 2 rows of 5 (slots A–10).
    Your Turn Draw from the deck or take the top discard card (only if you can use it).
    Number Cards (A–10) Place it in the matching slot. The face-down card that was there becomes your next card — keep going until you can't place!
    Jack — Wildcard Place in any empty slot you choose.
    King — Corner Wildcard Place in an empty corner slot only (A, 5, 6, or 10).
    Queen — Dead Card Discard it and end your turn immediately.
    Chain Ends When… The flipped card has no valid slot → discard it, turn over.
    All Default rules apply, plus: Play with 11 slots instead of 10.
    The ★ Slot An extra 11th slot that can only be filled by an Ace. Wildcards cannot go here.
    Wildcard Overlay If a wildcard sits in a slot, you can play the natural card on top of it. The displaced wildcard becomes your next chain card and gets re-evaluated as a wildcard.
    Win Condition First player to fill all 11 slots wins.
    Goal Win all 52 cards.
    Setup Deck is split evenly — 26 cards each, face-down.
    Each Round Both players flip their top card. Higher rank wins both cards (Ace is highest).
    War! If both cards are the same rank: each player puts 3 cards face-down, then flips 1 face-up. Higher face-up wins all cards. Ties repeat.
    Win Game ends when one player has all 52 cards.
    Goal Move all 52 cards to the 4 foundation piles (A→K by suit).
    Tableau 7 columns of cascading cards. Build down in alternating colors (red on black, black on red).
    Foundations Build up by suit from Ace to King. Double-tap a card to auto-send it.
    Stock Tap the deck to flip one card to the waste pile. Tap a waste card to select it, then tap a valid destination.
    Moving Cards Tap a face-up card to select it, then tap where you want it. Only Kings can go on empty columns.
    Goal Score 7 goals to win. Swipe your cat paw to hit the yarn ball into your opponent's goal.
    Controls Touch and drag anywhere to move your paw paddle. Your paw stays in your half of the table.
    Scoring Hit the yarn ball into the goal zone at your opponent's end. First to 7 wins!
    Mouse NPC A mouse occasionally runs across the table. If the yarn hits it, the ball deflects in a random direction!
    Tips Move your paw into the ball to give it momentum. A faster-moving paw hits harder. Defend your goal and look for angles!
    Goal Draw a shape from memory and have the app score how close your drawing is to the original!
    Preview You get 3 seconds to study a shape outline. Memorize it!
    Draw Sketch the shape on paper within the time limit — no peeking at the screen! Tap "Done Drawing" if you finish early.
    Scan Take a photo of your drawing with your camera. The app compares it to the original shape.
    Score Each player gets a similarity percentage. The closer your drawing, the higher your score!
    Winning Highest score wins the round. Win the most rounds to win the game. Choose 1, 3, or 5 rounds when creating a room.
    Goal Find and identify all 3 ghosts haunting the area by spelling their name on the Ouija board.
    Movement Use the virtual joystick (left half of screen) to move your investigator around the map. Ghosts roam freely — track them down!
    Tools Tap the tool buttons to switch between your Flashlight (cone of light), EMF Detector (radial signal), and Sound Recorder (wide ambient detection).
    Finding a Ghost Get close to a ghost and shine your flashlight directly at it. A strong signal (bar fills up) means you've located it — the ghost will be revealed on the map for your whole team!
    Ouija Board Stand near a found ghost and tap "Place Board" to open the Ouija minigame. Slide your finger over the letters — your phone will vibrate on key letters. The ghost spells its name… with diversions based on its personality!
    Ghost Personalities Shy ghosts hide and flee. Dramatic ghosts put on a show. Goofy ghosts add extra fake letters. Grumpy ghosts rush through quickly. Regal ghosts move slowly. Confused ghosts wander randomly. Personality affects name style and patience!
    Naming the Ghost After the Ouija session, type what you think the ghost's name is and submit. Correct = ghost identified! Wrong = it escapes to a new spot. Find and name all 3 to win!
    Multiplayer Play solo or cooperatively with up to 4 friends. All players share the same map — ghosts found by anyone are visible to everyone!
